# StreamSuites-Creator v0.5.0-alpha

`StreamSuites-Creator v0.5.0-alpha` focuses on creator tooling maturation (especially Wheels and Triggers), account identity management, and continued surface parity with the rest of the platform.

## 🎡 Wheels

- Complete creator wheel system delivered: editor, import/export (`.sswheel`), public links, sound assets, SSE live updates, and default vs scoreboard display modes.
- Public wheel consumption now properly linked from creator management.

## 🔔 Triggers & Livechat

- Creator custom trigger management with runtime-backed editor, validation, dry-run simulator, and platform chip presentation.
- Built-in commands, planned modules, and Pilled status clearly communicated as staged.

## 👤 Account & Identity

- Public identity alias management and secondary identity self-service tools.
- Scoped livechat progression settings and advanced roll-up suppression.

## 🎨 UX & Parity

- Account settings polish, social links editor scale, hovercard consistency.
- Rumble integration improvements and graceful degradation on partial data.
